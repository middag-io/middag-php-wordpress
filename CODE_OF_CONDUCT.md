# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and productive environment for maintainers, contributors, and users.

Participants are expected to:

- Use welcoming and respectful language.
- Be considerate of differing viewpoints and levels of experience.
- Give and receive constructive feedback in good faith.
- Focus discussions on the project, the code, and the documented goals.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about scope, priorities, and project direction.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith participation.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, commits, documentation, community channels, and maintainer interactions related to the project.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainers at:

<opensource@middag.io>

Reports should include relevant context, links, screenshots, or logs when available. Maintainers will review reports in good faith and handle them with appropriate confidentiality.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, including clarification, warning, temporary restriction, removal of comments, closing issues or pull requests, or banning a participant from project spaces.

Enforcement decisions are made to protect the health of the project and the people participating in it.
